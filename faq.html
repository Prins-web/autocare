Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AutoCare services, booking, pricing and safety

General Questions

What is AutoCare?

AutoCare is a technology-driven car service platform that connects vehicle owners with verified garages and trained professionals. We make car servicing simple, transparent, and hassle-free.

Is AutoCare available in my city?

AutoCare is currently expanding across major cities. You can check availability by entering your location while booking a service.

Booking & Pricing

How do I book a service?

You can book a service through our website by selecting the required service, entering vehicle details, choosing a date and confirming your booking.

Are prices fixed or can they change?

Prices shown are indicative. If additional repairs are required after inspection, we always take your approval before proceeding.

Do you charge extra for pickup & drop?

Pickup and drop are free for most services. Any exceptions will be clearly communicated during booking.

Safety & Trust

Are the garages verified?

Yes. All AutoCare partner garages are background-checked, quality-audited, and continuously monitored for service standards.

Do you use genuine spare parts?

We use genuine or OEM-approved spare parts depending on the service and customer preference to ensure reliability and performance.

Is there any warranty on services?

Yes. Most services come with a service or parts warranty. The warranty details depend on the type of service booked.

Safety & Trust

Is my vehicle safe with AutoCare?

Absolutely. Your vehicle is handled only by trained professionals and stored at verified partner garages with safety protocols in place.

Can I track my service status?

Yes. Our support team keeps you updated throughout the service process. In future updates, live tracking will be available.

Payments & Support

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept online payments, UPI, cards, and cash depending on location and service type.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support through phone, email, or WhatsApp. Our team is available to assist you before and after service.

Still Have Questions?

Our support team is always here to help you.
